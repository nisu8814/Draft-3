About Cosmos

Origins

Since our beginnings in 2001, we at Cosmo's pizza have prided ourselves in hand crafting true deck oven pizza, made with the finest and freshest ingredients available.

At Cosmo's, we shred our cheese and top our vegetables daily. We make our dough in shop every day. We believe that the extra time our skilled pizza crafters take to craft a true deck oven pizza with the highest quality ingredients leads to a greater customer experience.

Our Mission Statement

We also believe that details matter, and if you choose delivery, you can always count on a dedicated Cosmo's delivery professional. At Cosmo's we don't cut corners, and we believe that makes all the difference.

Finally, we strive towards the highest standards in Customer Service, Consistency, and Cleanliness. We proudly serve our valued customers from our six locations in Boulder, Denver, Lafayette and Fort Collins.
